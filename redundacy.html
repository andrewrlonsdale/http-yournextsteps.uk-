Redundancy Services

John offers Outplacement Services to employees facing redundancy in Edinburgh, Glasgow and Central Scotland.

John can provide honest advice on how staff can showcase their skills and best market themselves for the future. His experience covers most professions from trainees to senior executives.

    Redundancy Services include:

    • Face to Face interviews
    • Professional CV Writing Service
    • LinkedIn Profile Writing
    • Interview Coaching
    • Advice and Guidance on where to look for jobs
    • Guidance on Aptitude Tests and Assessment Centres
    • Speculative mailshots and networking
    • Building confidence to enable employees to ‘sell’ their skills

The service is bespoke and is more personal and targeted towards the individual than many other Outplacement companies.
